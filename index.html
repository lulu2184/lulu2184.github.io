About

Search and Rescue Incident Dashboard (SaRID) help you to easily share, report, analyze and manage data about an incident.

No more delayed, inaccurate, and/or lost information!

Marker Generation

No need to look through FEMA marking standard.

SaRID can remind you of the information you need for a marker and generate the marker for you.

Reporting

No need to refresh everything from memory.

SaRID can generate a report from data collected during the operation.

Search Status Visualization

No need to move pins on paper map.

SaRID can show you current markers on the map and give you real time updates.

Team management

No need to rely on unstable radio.

SaRID can provide you an efficient way to assign members to teams and assign teams to tasks.
